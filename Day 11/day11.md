Day 11 - ATS Resume Optimization

What I Learned:-

Today I learned how Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) evaluate resumes before they reach recruiters.

I explored how ATS software scans resumes for keywords, skills, and job relevance.

This activity helped me understand the importance of resume optimization and keyword alignment for improving job application success.

My Work:-

- Uploaded my resume for ATS analysis.
- Selected an AI Engineer Job Description.
- Compared resume content with job requirements.
- Reviewed ATS Match Score and Gap Analysis.
- Identified missing keywords and skills.
- Generated an ATS-optimized resume.
- Downloaded the optimized resume.
- Captured screenshots of ATS analysis and final resume.

Key Learnings:-

- ATS systems filter resumes before recruiter review.
- Keyword alignment improves resume visibility.
- Resume structure impacts ATS readability.
- Gap analysis helps identify missing skills.
- AI can optimize resumes while maintaining factual accuracy.

Conclusion:-

This task helped me understand how ATS systems work and how resumes can be optimized for better recruiter visibility. 

I learned the importance of keyword matching, professional formatting, and targeted resume customization for specific job roles.
