Interview Preparation Guide

🎯 Most Likely Interview Questions

Q: Walk me through your most complex ML project.

Answer: Describe the end-to-end process—problem definition, data collection/preprocessing, model selection, hyperparameter tuning, evaluation metrics, and business impact.

Q: How do you approach model optimization?

Answer: Discuss hyperparameter tuning, regularization techniques (dropout, L1/L2), cross-validation, feature selection, and measurement of improvements using appropriate metrics.

Q: Tell me about your experience with deep learning frameworks.

Answer: Detail specific projects using TensorFlow and Keras, architectures implemented (CNN, RNN), and optimization approaches used.

Q: How do you handle data preprocessing?

Answer: Mention normalization, standardization, handling missing values, categorical encoding, feature engineering, and EDA practices.

Q: What's your experience deploying AI models to production?

Answer: Discuss dashboard applications, model integration into web interfaces, and real-time inference handling. Mention learning opportunities in deployment frameworks.
